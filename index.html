Who I Am

Precision cutting.

I'm Imad-Eddine Youcef Merabti — a freelance video editor based in Algeria, cutting exclusively in DaVinci Resolve. I work with creators and brands on content that doesn't just look clean, but actually performs.

Short-form, UGC, paid ads, long-form — each format has its own retention logic and I treat them differently. Every frame I cut has a reason to be there. I also coach editors on DaVinci Resolve, which means I understand content strategy deeply enough to go beyond the cut when it helps you.
